How to Ask
The Gift Edit

She said "nothing." She lied. 🎁

From splurge to handmade — something for every kind of love.

💐

Flowers Before 9am

Ferns N Petals, local market, or Bloom Box. Delivered before she wakes up. No excuses.

👜

A Bag She'll Actually Use

Not another tote. A leather pouch, structured handbag, or good weekender. Hidesign, Nappa Dori, Da Milano — solid at every price point.

💍

Jewellery That Means Something

A birthstone, a thin chain, a ring she'll wear every day. Misho, Zariin, or a local goldsmith. Ask what she actually wears first.

✉️

A Handwritten Card

Buy a beautiful card from a paper store. One honest sentence she'll keep in a drawer for twenty years. No templates. Write it yourself.

🎨

Make It With Your Child

Clay pendant: roll, shape, paint.
Decorated frame: pasta, dried flowers, her favourite photo.
Recipe card: her dish, handwritten by the kids, framed.
Photo book: Canvera or Printinkart, from ₹500.

🖼️

Put Her in the Frame

Print and frame a photo where she's actually in it. The whole of her. Hand it to her. That's the note.

☕

A Hamper She'd Love

Her favourite biscuits, a good tea, a candle, a face mask. Wrap it in tissue and brown paper. The effort is the gift.

🕯️

A Quiet Evening

You cook. You clean. She sits with her chai and watches what she actually wants. That's the whole gift.
